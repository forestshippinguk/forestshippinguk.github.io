How Can a Customs Warehouse Help You?

Forest Shipping UK is in the process of applying for Public Customs Warehouse authorization, and we're excited to help your business thrive. A customs warehouse is a powerful tool that allows businesses to store imported goods without paying VAT or Duties upfront. This gives you more flexibility to manage your cash flow and timing, keeping your operations smooth and cost-efficient.

Key Benefits:

  • Defer VAT & Duty Payments – Improve Your Cash Flow
    One of the biggest advantages is the ability to delay VAT and Duty payments until your goods are ready for sale. This extra time allows you to keep your working capital available, making it easier to manage cash flow and maintain a healthy bottom line.
  • Order Larger Quantities for Better Pricing
    With a customs warehouse, you can place larger orders with suppliers without the worry of immediate tax liabilities. This helps you take advantage of bulk pricing discounts, which can reduce your overall costs and improve your profitability.
  • Faster Response to Market Demands – Always Have UK Stock Ready
    By storing your goods in the UK, you'll always have stock on hand to quickly meet market demands. This reduces lead times, helps you stay ahead of competitors, and ensures you're always ready to fulfill customer orders promptly.
